Odoo Custom Modules

We have designed and customized multiple Odoo modules tailored for law firms and intellectual property management. These modules integrate legal case management, trademark, and recordal processes into one centralized ERP solution.

Legal Case Management System

This module is designed for **law firms** and automates the complete lifecycle of legal cases. It allows lawyers and firms to track case details, manage clients, schedule hearings, and maintain all necessary documents.

  • Client & Party Management (Corporate & Individual)
  • Case Registration & Categorization
  • Case Stages & Hearings Scheduling
  • Complete Document Management (FIRs, Orders, Judgements)
  • Lawyer Assignment & Team Collaboration
  • Automated Reminders & Notifications
  • Custom Analytics & Reports

CFMS Trademark & IP Management System

Initially developed by UET Software Department, this system was **enhanced and customized** for company-specific needs. It now includes additional workflows for trademark, patent, design, copyright, and recordal.

  • Trademark Multi-Stage Tracking
  • Patent Filing & Renewals
  • Copyright & Design Registration
  • **Recordal Module** (newly added)
  • Customized Dashboards & KPIs
  • Workflow Optimization & Reporting
